Envision Portal sends in-app notifications to keep you informed about activity relevant to your datasets and requests.

## Inbox

Your notifications are collected in the inbox at `/app/inbox`. Unread notifications are indicated in the sidebar.

## Notification Types

| Type | When it is sent |
| --- | --- |
| New access request | Someone has submitted a request for one of your datasets |
| Request status update | The status of a request you submitted has changed |
| Dataset invitation | You have been invited to join a dataset as a team member |
| DUA update | A data use agreement has been signed or updated on a controlled request |

## Managing Notifications

From the inbox you can:

- **Mark as read** - Dismiss a notification after reviewing it
- **Delete** - Remove a notification permanently

Notifications are also sent by email for important events. The email address used is the one on your account profile.
